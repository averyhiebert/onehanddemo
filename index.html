One-Handed Typing Demo (WIP)



Instructions

When Caps lock is off, type normally.

When Caps Lock is on, type with your left hand as though it was your right hand (see Randall Munroe's blog post, my original inspiration, for an explanation of the concept). The webapp will try to guess which keys you intended without you needing to specify. Use shift+space for a newline, and ` (backtick) to backspace.

When in Caps Lock mode you can only enter & delete text at the end of the text. Sorry.

Note that currently I map a to ;, not ' as Randall does, but I will be adding support for alternate layouts in the future. Note also that my mapping (and Randall's) differs slightly from another commonly taught finger layout for touch typing (as described by the creator of similar product One Hand Keyboard here).
