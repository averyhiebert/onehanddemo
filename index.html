One-Handed Typing Demo (WIP)



Instructions

When Caps lock is off, type normally.

When Caps Lock is on, type with your left hand as though it was your right hand (see Randall Munroe's blog post, my original inspiration, for an explanation of the concept). The webapp will try to guess which keys you intended without you needing to specify. The key mapping is based on Randall's mapping, but extended to cover both hands. Use shift+space for a newline, and ` (backtick) to backspace.

When in Caps Lock mode you can only enter & delete text at the end of the text. Sorry.

Note also that this mapping differs slightly from another commonly taught finger layout for touch typing (as described by the creator of similar product One Hand Keyboard here).
